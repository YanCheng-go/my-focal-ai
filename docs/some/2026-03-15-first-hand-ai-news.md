## Post

Every AI news platform shows you what the crowd cares about. Not what you care about.

I got tired of scrolling through recycled announcements and hot takes, so I built my own feed.

MyFocalAI (https://myfocalai.vercel.app) lets you pick your own sources. I picked those for myself: X(twitter) posts from engineers/builders like Karpathy, arXiv papers, engineering blogs leading companies, YouTube bloggers for long tutorial videos or podcasts, model leaderboards, tech events, GitHub trending repos, Claude Code release notes.

An LLM then scores every item against principles you define. Mine are: signal over noise, mechanisms over opinions, builders over commentators. You write yours.

It runs locally (or with Ollama's LLMs for free scoring feature), or you can view it online anytime here: https://myfocalai.vercel.app (login to set up your feed sources)

Open source: https://github.com/YanCheng-go/my-focal-ai
